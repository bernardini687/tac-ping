I'm logging my cat's thoughts!
I know how it sounds, allow me to explain.

My name's Freya Copson and I'm a young developer.
I share a flat with my fat cat Tac.

Put simply, somewhen around november 2018 I hacked my Ping, a penguin-shaped, domestic robot, produced by the company I work for. Now it can detect Tac's thoughts as raw data and translate it to English.

But the really cool part is that Ping can immediately understand Tac and vice versa.

So, of course, I program specific questions I want Ping to ask Tac while I'm gone. I'd love to live chat with my cat but, for technical reasons, Ping's detection capabilities suck when I'm around, and the translation process can take up to a couple of days.

Anyway, I was so impressed by the depth of my cat's mind that I decided to share it with the wide world.

For the sake of clarity and aesthetics, I edited the logs of their conversations in a dialogic form, posted a few of my favourites, and called it a season.

Tac&Ping Season 1 was live!

Expect a Season 2 soon, those two are really getting along.

May their minds blow up yours:
